## Business

### Navigation
- [Business Sidebar](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-sidebar.md): The main navigation menu for the business portal — accessible on every page, contains links to all business features

### Onboarding & Profile
- [Business Profile Setup](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-profile-setup.md): Set up your business investment profile and configure matching criteria to connect with compatible investors
- [Business Profile](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-profile.md): View and manage your personal and business profile details, referral link, and investor visibility settings

### Dashboard
- [Business Dashboard](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-dashboard.md): Your central hub for tracking investor readiness scores, viewing investor matches, accessing investment assets, and managing premium services

### Investor Readiness Assessments
- [Investor Eligibility](https://capital-connect.github.io/investor-eligibility.md): Assess whether your business meets the criteria investors look for — covers fundraising preparedness, investor readiness, and financing considerations
- [Investor Preparedness](https://capital-connect.github.io/investor-preparedness.md): Assess how operationally and strategically ready your business is to receive and manage investment
- [Business Factsheet](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-factsheet.md): Build a structured financial and operational summary of your business covering current performance, forecasts, and compliance — used as an investor-facing factsheet
- [Impact Assessment](https://capital-connect.github.io/impact-assessment.md): Measure and communicate the social, environmental, and governance (ESG) impact your business creates — improves matching with impact-focused investors

### Investor Discovery
- [Investor Database](https://capital-connect.github.io/investor-database.md): Browse and filter the global investor database, view matching criteria analytics, and explore investor profiles matched to your business
- [Advisor Database](https://capital-connect.github.io/advisor-database.md): Discover and connect with transaction advisors who can support your fundraising journey — browse by sector, country, and advisory type

### AI Tools
- [AI Profile Analysis](https://capital-connect.github.io/ai-profile-analysis.md): Use Lara AI to analyze your investor readiness profile, identify funding gaps, review your documents, and get a prioritized action plan — free of charge

### Capital Raising Materials
- [Advisories](https://capital-connect.github.io/advisories.md): View, book, and manage advisory sessions with expert consultants to get personalized fundraising guidance
- [Financials](https://capital-connect.github.io/financials.md): Enter and manage your business financial statements — Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow — to support investor due diligence and business valuation
- [My Documents](https://capital-connect.github.io/my-documents.md): Upload and manage your investment documents — organized into folders for Business Teaser, Business Valuation, Pitch Deck, and Financial Model

### Premium Services
- [Business Valuation](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-valuation.md): Request a data-powered business valuation report benchmarked against 167k+ global transactions — delivered in minutes
- [One Time Services](https://capital-connect.github.io/one-time-services.md): Explore and purchase premium one-time services including Business Valuation ($99.99) and Advisory Sessions ($59.99)

### Applications & Funding Calls
- [Business Call for Application](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-call-for-application.md): Browse and apply for open funding calls — both global opportunities and those matched to your business profile — and track your application status

### Billing
- [Business Invoices](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-invoices.md): View and download invoices for all payments made on the platform, including valuation reports and advisory sessions
