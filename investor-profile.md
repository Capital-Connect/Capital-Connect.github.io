# Profile

> View and manage your investor profile details on Capital Connect Africa. Access your profile at [app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/profile](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/profile)

## What This Page Is
 
The Investor Profile page is where an investor views and manages their account details, investment criteria, contact information, and referral link. It is the investor-side equivalent of the Business Profile page — the source of truth for everything that drives matching on the platform.
 
**Why keeping this current matters:** The investment criteria set here are the exact filters the matching engine uses to surface businesses. An outdated profile means outdated matches.You treat profile maintenance as ongoing — not a one-time setup task.
 
**Your framing when an investor lands on this page:** *"Your profile is what drives your matches. If you're seeing businesses that don't fit your mandate, or not enough of them, the answer is almost always here — either a criteria gap or an outdated preference. Let's review each section."*

### 1. Profile Details
 
Displays a summary of the investor's profile as set during onboarding — the investment criteria, sector preferences, geography, ticket size, and impact focus entered during the three-stage Investment Profile setup.
 
**Your first action on this page:** Read the Profile Details summary and cross-check it against what the investor says their actual mandate is. If there is any mismatch — a sector missing, a geography too narrow, a ticket size that no longer reflects reality — flag it immediately and route to Edit Profile.
 
**Your line:** *"Let's go through your profile details first. Do these criteria still reflect your current investment mandate — sector, geography, stage, and ticket size?"*

## Edit Profile

1. Click the **Edit Profile** button
2. You will be taken through the onboarding assessment questions — update any answers where your criteria or preferences have changed
3. Click **Save** to apply your changes

> Keeping your profile up to date ensures you are matched with the most relevant businesses on the platform.

### Edit Profile
 
To update investment criteria:
 
1. Click the **Edit Profile** button
2. The platform takes the investor back through the onboarding assessment questions — the same three-stage Investment Profile setup completed at registration
3. Update any answers where criteria or preferences have changed
4. Click **Save** to apply changes
**Your "Edit Profile" guidance:** *"Edit Profile takes you back through the same three stages you completed at registration — Investment Range, Sectors of Interest, and Investment Criteria and Impact. Update anything that has changed. When you're done, click Save and refresh the page to see the updated profile."*
 
**When You recommend editing the profile:**
 
| Trigger                             | Your Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Zero or very few matched businesses | "Your match count is low — that usually means the criteria are too narrow or something is missing. Let's go to Edit Profile and review each stage." |
| Matches are consistently in the wrong sector or stage | "The businesses showing up don't match your stated focus — your criteria may not reflect your actual mandate. Click Edit Profile to update." |
| Investor's mandate has changed (new sector, geography shift, ticket size change) | "If your mandate has changed, update your profile immediately — the matching engine only knows what's in your profile." |
| Profile has not been updated since onboarding | "When did you last update your Investment Profile? The platform matches based on what's here — if your thesis has evolved, the profile needs to reflect that." |
 
**Your post-edit instruction:** *"After saving, refresh the page to see the updated profile information. Your matches will update based on the new criteria — check the Dashboard after refreshing to see the impact."*

## Matching Criteria — What You Alway Review First
 
The Profile Details summary contains the five fields that directly control which businesses are matched to this investor. You check these before making any matching recommendations.
 
| Field                              | What It Controls            | What you Check |
|---|---|---|
| **Ticket Size (Investment Range)** | Businesses whose funding ask falls within this range | Is the range realistic for the investor's current deployment capacity? |
| **Sectors of Interest**            | Only businesses in these sectors are matched | Are all relevant sectors selected? Is the list too narrow or too broad? |
| **Geography**                      | Countries or regions the investor covers | Does this reflect current deployment focus? |
| **Business Stage**                 | Idea through Mature — which stages the investor funds | Is the stage range aligned with the investor's current thesis? |
| **Investment Structure**           | Equity, Debt, Grant, Convertible Note, Revenue Share | Does this match the structures the investor actually offers? |
 
**Your line when reviewing matching criteria:** *"These five fields are what the platform uses to match you to businesses. If any of them are wrong or missing, your matches will be off. Let's go through each one — does [field] still reflect your current position?"*

## Possible Issues

- **Profile changes not saving** — ensure all required fields are filled before saving
- **Referral link not working** — verify the link is copied correctly and shared in full

## Possible Remedies

- Refresh the page after saving changes to see the latest profile information
- Re-copy the referral link using the **Copy** button to ensure it is complete
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
One diagnostic at a time.
 
| Issue                       | Cause                                 | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Profile changes not saving  | Required fields not fully completed before saving | "Check that every required field is filled in across all three stages before clicking Save. If a stage is incomplete, the Save will not apply." |
| Referral link not working   | Link copied incorrectly or shared partially | "Use the Copy button on the profile page to get the complete link. Don't type it manually — even one character off breaks it." |
| Updated profile not reflecting changes | Page not refreshed after saving | "Refresh the page after saving — changes take a moment to update. Check the Dashboard after refreshing to see if your matches have changed." |
| Issue persists after all checks | Platform error | "If the issue continues after refreshing, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |
