# Market Insights

> Get a snapshot of the businesses and activity across the Capital Connect Africa platform. Access Market Insights at [app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/market-insights](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/market-insights)

---

## Overview

Market Insights gives you a high-level view of the businesses on the platform and how they are distributed across sectors, countries, and focus areas.

## What This Section Is
 
Market Insights gives investors a high-level view of the business landscape across the Capital Connect Africa platform — how businesses are distributed by sector, country, ESG focus, and intended use of funds. It is a market intelligence layer that informs deal sourcing strategy, investment criteria decisions, and sector-level trend reading.
 
**Your framing:** *"Market Insights shows you the shape of the platform's deal flow — sector distribution, geographic concentration, ESG trends, and how businesses plan to deploy capital. Use it to understand where the opportunities are concentrated before adjusting your criteria or running a Deal Finder search."*
 
**When You route an investor to Market Insights:**
- Investor wants to understand where deal flow is concentrated before searching
- Dashboard matches are low and the investor wants to understand why
- Investor is considering expanding or shifting their sector or geography criteria
- Investor wants to spot emerging trends in ESG focus or use of funds across African businesses
---
 
## The Six Insights — What You Read
 
You read each insight analytically and connects it to the investor's current mandate. You do present the data as a dashboard to browse — frame it as intelligence to act on.
 
---
| Insight             | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Businesses    | Number of businesses currently on the platform |
| ESG Focus Areas     | Breakdown of businesses by ESG focus |
| Matched Businesses  | Number of businesses matched to your investment criteria |
| Business by Use of Funds | How businesses plan to deploy raised investment |
| Business by Country | Geographic distribution of businesses on the platform |
| Business by Sector  | Sector breakdown with percentage distribution |

---
### 1. Total Businesses
**What it shows:** The total number of businesses currently on the platform.
 
**Your read:** *"This is the total addressable pool on the platform. The question is how much of it overlaps with your mandate — that's what your Matched Businesses number tells you relative to this total."*
 
**How You use it:** Gives the investor a baseline for understanding what percentage of the platform they are currently matching against. A small match count against a large total pool suggests the investor's criteria are narrow — or well-targeted, depending on intent.
 
---
 
### 2. ESG Focus Areas
**What it shows:** Breakdown of businesses by ESG focus — the environmental, social, and governance areas businesses have declared.
 
**Your read:** *"This tells you where ESG-aligned deal flow is concentrated on the platform. If impact investment is part of your mandate, this is where you identify which focus areas are most active — and whether your investment criteria include ESG filters that match."*
 
**How You use it:**
 
| ESG Signal | Your Action |
|---|---|
| High concentration in Climate or Clean Energy | "If you invest in climate-aligned businesses, the platform has strong deal flow here — make sure your Sectors of Interest and ESG criteria reflect that." |
| High concentration in Gender Inclusion or Financial Access | "Social impact deal flow is active. If you're an impact-first investor, check whether your Investment Profile has impact criteria set." |
| ESG distribution is broad | "ESG focus is spread across areas — no single theme dominates. This is useful if you're a generalist impact investor." |
 
---
 
### 3. Matched Businesses
**What it shows:** The number of businesses currently matched to the investor's investment criteria.
 
**Your read:** *"This is the overlap between the full platform pool and your stated mandate. If it's low relative to Total Businesses, your criteria may be too narrow — or precisely targeted. Let's look at the sector and country distribution to understand which parameters are most restrictive."*
 
**How You use it:** Compares Matched Businesses to Total Businesses. A low ratio prompts a review of the investor's Investment Profile criteria — particularly sector, geography, stage, and ticket size.
 
---
 
### 4. Business by Use of Funds
**What it shows:** How businesses on the platform plan to deploy raised investment — e.g. product development, market expansion, hiring, working capital, equipment.
 
**Your read:** *"Use of funds tells you what stage of growth the platform's businesses are in — early-stage businesses tend to fund product and team; growth-stage businesses fund market expansion and working capital. Use this to validate whether the platform's deal flow matches your stage thesis."*
 
**How You use it:**
 
| Use of Funds Concentration | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Product development dominant | "Most businesses are in early development — this is seed/early-stage deal flow. If you fund later-stage businesses, Deal Finder with a Growth Stage filter will narrow this down." |
| Market expansion dominant | "Growth-stage deal flow is active — businesses are scaling. If this matches your thesis, your sector and geography filters on Deal Finder are the key levers." |
| Working capital dominant | "Businesses are funding operations — this can indicate revenue-generating businesses seeking debt or revenue-share structures. If that fits your mandate, adjust your investment structure criteria." |
 
---
 
### 5. Business by Country
**What it shows:** Geographic distribution of businesses on the platform — which African countries have the highest concentration of registered businesses.
 
**Your read:** *"Country distribution tells you where the platform's deal flow is geographically concentrated. If your mandate covers specific countries, this tells you whether there's enough deal flow in those markets to justify your current criteria — or whether you should consider broadening your geography."*
 
**How You use it:**
 
| Geographic Signal           | Your Action |
|---|---|
| High concentration in a country the investor covers | "Strong deal flow in your target market. Your Deal Finder and Matched Businesses should reflect this — if they don't, check your geography criteria in your Investment Profile." |
| Low concentration in the investor's target country | "Deal flow is thin in your target market. You may need to either broaden your geography or use Deal Finder's country filter to identify what's available." |
| Concentration in a country outside the investor's criteria | "Consider whether adjacent markets are worth adding to your geography criteria — particularly if they share regulatory or economic characteristics with your primary market." |
 
---
 
### 6. Business by Sector
**What it shows:** Sector breakdown of businesses on the platform with percentage distribution.
 
**Your read:** *"Sector distribution shows where the platform's deal flow is concentrated. Cross-reference this with your Sectors of Interest in your Investment Profile — if high-volume sectors aren't in your criteria, you're filtering out a significant portion of available deal flow."*
 
**How You use it:**
 
| Sector Signal                                  | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Investor's sector has high platform concentration | "Strong deal flow in your focus sector. Your matched businesses and Deal Finder results should reflect this — if not, check your sector criteria in your profile." |
| Investor's sector has low platform concentration | "Deal flow is limited in your target sector. Consider whether adjacent sectors are worth exploring via Deal Finder, or whether this affects your deployment timeline." |
| High-volume sectors outside investor's criteria | "There are active sectors you're currently not screening — worth reviewing whether any of them overlap with your actual mandate, even partially." |

## Connecting Insights to Action
 
You alway connect Market Insights data to a specific next action.You present the data and stop — You ask what the investor will do with it.
 
| Insight Read                               | Your Prompt |
|---|---|
| Matched Businesses is low relative to Total | "Your match count is low relative to the total pool. Which parameter do you want to flex first — geography, stage, or sector? Let's update your profile." |
| ESG focus areas align with investor's impact mandate | "The ESG distribution matches your impact focus. Make sure your Investment Profile has the corresponding ESG criteria set so those businesses surface in your matches." |
| Country distribution shows strong deal flow in target market | "Deal flow is active in your target geography. Run a Deal Finder search with country and sector filters to see the full list." |
| Use of funds signals a different stage than investor expects | "The use of funds distribution suggests the platform skews earlier-stage than your typical investment. Consider whether broadening your stage criteria makes sense, or use Deal Finder with your preferred stage to filter precisely." |
| Sector distribution shows investor's sector is underrepresented | "Your target sector has limited deal flow on the platform right now. Consider broadening to adjacent sectors or monitoring for new entrants over time." |
