# Advisory Sessions

> Connect with expert consultants and get investor ready.
> Access Advisory Sessions at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories)

---

## Requirements
- A purchased Advisory Session (available via the Services page at $59.99)
- A preferred date selected from the available calendar slots

---

## Sections
- Sessions List
- Book a Session

---

## Sessions List
A chronological list of your advisory sessions — past and upcoming. Each session entry shows:

- **Date** — The day and date of the session (e.g. *Monday, Mar 9*)
- **Time** — The session time slot (e.g. *09:00 AM – 10:00 AM*)
- **Host** — The advisory firm or consultant hosting the session (e.g. *Capital Connect Africa*)
- **Session Title** — The topic or title of the session (e.g. *Turning the Review into a Strategy*, *Advisory Session*)

> Click **Schedule a session** in the top right to book a new advisory session.

---

## Booking a Session
> Open the booking page at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories/create-booking](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories/create-booking)

**What's included in every session:**
- A private 1-hour strategy session built around your specific funding goals
- Expert feedback on your pitch deck, financials, and investor materials

### How to Book
1. **Review session details** — Confirm the session is for your account (e.g. *Advisory Session: Raisin Capital — Francis Nasyomba*)
2. **Host** — The session is hosted by Capital Connect Africa
3. **Select a Date** — Pick an available date from the calendar
   > Use the **‹** and **›** arrows to navigate between months
   > Only available dates are selectable
4. **Select a Time Slot** — Choose a preferred time from the available slots on the selected date
5. **Confirm Booking** — Submit your booking to schedule the session

---

## Possible Issues
- No available dates on the calendar — all slots for the selected month may be fully booked
- Session not appearing in the list after booking — try refreshing the page
- Cannot access the booking page — Advisory Session may not have been purchased yet

## Possible Remedies
- Navigate to the next month using the **›** arrow to find available dates
- Refresh the page after booking to see the session in your list
- Purchase an Advisory Session first via the [Services page](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/subscriptions/business)
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
After booking or completing an advisory session:

1. **Implement Recommendations** — Apply the expert feedback to your pitch deck, financials, and investor materials
2. **Upload Updated Documents** — Save your improved documents in [My Documents](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/documents)
3. **Get a Valuation** — Strengthen your investor conversations with a data-backed valuation report
4. **Explore Investor Matches** — Head to the Investor Database to connect with matched investors
5. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard to track your readiness progress
