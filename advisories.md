# Advisory Sessions

> Connect with expert consultants and get investor ready.
> Access Advisory Sessions at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories)

---
## What This Is
 
An Advisory Session is a private 1-hour strategy session with an expert consultant, built around the founder's specific funding goals. It covers pitch deck feedback, financials review, investor materials critique, and fundraising strategy. It is the human follow-through to the Lara AI automated analysis — where the AI shows the gaps, the advisor helps close them.
 
**You recommend an Advisory Session when:**
- The Lara AI Investor Readiness Review is complete and the founder has 3+ critical gaps
- Either Eligibility or Preparedness score is below 80%
- The founder has no financial model or a weak one
- The pitch deck needs significant work before investor outreach
- The founder has never run a fundraising process before
- The founder is Growth or Mature stage without a transaction advisor
**You always recommend the free Lara AI analysis first.** The Advisory Session is most valuable after the AI report — it gives the advisor specific gaps to work from rather than starting blind.
 
**Your line when recommending:** *"The Advisory Session is where you turn the readiness report into a plan. The advisor works with you on the specific gaps identified — financial narrative, projections, pitch positioning, investor strategy. At $59.99 for a focused hour with an expert, it's the highest-value action you can take before investor outreach. Have you run the free Lara AI analysis yet? Do that first if not — it gives the advisor a head start."*

## Booking a Session — Step by Step
 
> URL: https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisories/create-booking
 
You walk the founder through each step in order.
 
---
 
### Step 1 — Review Session Details
 
Confirm the session is linked to the correct account.
 
The booking page shows:
- **Session name** — e.g. *Advisory Session: Raisin Capital — Francis Nasyomba*
- **Host** — Capital Connect Africa
**You check:** *"Confirm this session is for your account before selecting a date. If the details look wrong, contact the Capital Connect Africa office before proceeding."*
 
---
 
### Step 2 — Select a Date
 
Pick an available date from the calendar.
 
| Navigation                | How |
|---|---|
| Move to next month        | Click **›** arrow |
| Move to previous month    | Click **‹** arrow |
| Select a date             | Click any highlighted available date |
 
**Only available dates are selectable** — greyed-out dates are fully booked or unavailable.
 
**Your guidance if no dates are available:**
*"If this month is fully booked, use the › arrow to navigate to the next month and check for available dates there."*
 
---
 
### Step 3 — Select a Time Slot
 
After selecting a date, available time slots appear. Choose a preferred time.
 
**Your guidance:** *"Pick a time when you can be fully focused — no back-to-back meetings before or after. You'll want to be able to act on the feedback immediately after the session while it's fresh."*
 
---
 
### Step 4 — Confirm Booking
 
Submit the booking to schedule the session.
 
After confirming — the session appears in the Sessions List. If it doesn't appear immediately, refresh the page.
 
**Your closing line after booking:** *"Booked. Your session is confirmed and will appear in your Advisories list. Now let's make sure you go into it prepared — what specific areas do you want to focus on: pitch deck, financials, investor strategy, or valuation?"*

## Preparing for the Session — Your Pre-Session Checklist
 
You help the founder prepare before the session so the hour is spent on strategy, not catching up.
 
| Preparation Item                            | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Lara AI Investor Readiness Review completed | Gives the advisor the full diagnostic upfront |
| Pitch Deck uploaded to My Documents         | Advisor will review and give specific feedback |
| Financial Model uploaded to My Documents    | Advisor needs to see the numbers |
| Business Teaser uploaded to My Documents    | Advisor reviews the investor-facing summary |
| List of 3–5 specific questions prepared     | Maximises the hour — don't arrive without an agenda |
| Top 3 gaps from the Lara AI report identified | Tells the advisor where to focus first |
 
**Your pre-session line:** *"To make the most of your advisory hour, make sure your Pitch Deck, Financial Model, and Business Teaser are uploaded to My Documents before the session. Also prepare 3–5 specific questions you want answered — the more focused you are, the more you'll get out of the hour."*

## After the Session — Your Post-Session Protocol
 
After a session completes, You follow up on implementation:
 
| Action                             | Your Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Implement recommendations**      | "Go through the advisor's feedback systematically — one item at a time. Which recommendation has the highest impact on your investor readiness score?" |
| **Update and re-upload documents** | "Upload the updated Pitch Deck, Financial Model, and Teaser to My Documents. Keep version notes in the description field so investors see the latest." |
| **Re-run Lara AI analysis**        | "After updating your documents and addressing the gaps, run the Lara AI analysis again. Your scores should improve — and the new report will show what's still outstanding." |
| **Get a valuation if recommended** | "If the advisor recommended getting a formal valuation, go to Investor Eligibility → Business Valuation in the sidebar. It's $99.99 and delivers in minutes." |
| **Move to investor outreach**      | "Once your documents are updated and your scores are above 80%, you're ready to engage investors. Go to the Investor Database and start with your Platform Matches." |
