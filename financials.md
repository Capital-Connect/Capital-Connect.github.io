# Financials

> Track and manage your business financial statements to support investor readiness and decision making.
> Financials are organized into three statements: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow.
> Access Financials at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials)

---

## Sections
- Income Statement
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow

> Each statement supports multiple years of data, displayed side by side for easy comparison.
> Currency defaults to **United States Dollar** and can be changed using the edit icon next to the currency label.
> Toggle **Edit Mode** on to enter or update figures directly in the table.

---

## Income Statement
> An income statement is a financial statement that shows the financial performance of a business over a period of time. It is used to track the financial performance of a business and to make decisions about the business.
> Access at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/income-statement](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/income-statement)

### Fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Revenue** | Total income generated by the business |
| **Advisory Services** | Revenue specifically from advisory services rendered |
| **Cost of Sales** | Direct costs attributable to the production of goods or services sold |
| **Gross Profit** | Revenue minus Cost of Sales |
| **Gross Margin** | Gross Profit as a percentage of Revenue |
| **Operating Expenses** | Total costs incurred in running the business |
| **Staff Costs** | Employee salaries and related expenses |
| **EBITDA** | Earnings Before Interest, Tax, Depreciation, and Amortisation |
| **EBITDA Margin** | EBITDA as a percentage of Revenue |
| **Depreciation & Amortisation** | Reduction in value of assets over time |
| **EBIT** | Earnings Before Interest and Tax |
| **Interests** | Interest expenses on debt |
| **Profit Before Tax** | Earnings before tax deductions |
| **Taxes** | Tax obligations for the period |
| **Net Profit** | Final profit after all deductions including tax |

> Figures shown in red indicate negative values (losses or deficits).

## Balance Sheet
> A balance sheet is a financial statement that shows the financial position of a business at a specific point in time. It is used to track the financial position of a business and to make decisions about the business.
> Access at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/balance-sheet](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/balance-sheet)

### Fields

**Assets**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Land & Property** | Value of land and property owned by the business |
| **Plant & Equipment** | Value of machinery, tools, and equipment |
| **Other Non-Current Assets** | Long-term assets not classified elsewhere |
| **Trade Receivables** | Amounts owed to the business by customers |
| **Cash** | Cash and cash equivalents held by the business |
| **Inventory** | Value of goods held for sale |
| **Other Current Assets** | Short-term assets not classified elsewhere |
| **Total Assets** | Sum of all assets |

**Liabilities**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Trade Payables** | Amounts owed by the business to suppliers |
| **Other Current Liabilities** | Short-term obligations not classified elsewhere |
| **Loans** | Outstanding loan balances |
| **Other Non-Current Liabilities** | Long-term obligations not classified elsewhere |
| **Total Liabilities** | Sum of all liabilities |

**Equity**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Capital** | Funds invested by owners or shareholders |
| **Other Reserves** | Retained reserves not classified elsewhere |
| **Retained Earnings** | Cumulative profits retained in the business |
| **Dividends** | Distributions paid to shareholders |
| **Shareholders Equity** | Total equity attributable to shareholders |

---

## Cash Flow
> Cash flow is a financial statement that shows the movement of cash in and out of a business. It is used to track the cash flow of a business and to make decisions about the business.
> Access at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/cash-flows](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/financials/cash-flows)

### Fields

**Operating Activities**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Profit Before Tax** | Earnings before tax — starting point for cash flow calculation |
| **Depreciation & Amortisation** | Non-cash expense added back to profit |
| **Taxes Paid** | Actual tax payments made during the period |
| **Operating Cash Flow** | Net cash generated from core business operations |

**Working Capital Changes**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Change in Receivables** | Movement in amounts owed to the business |
| **Change in Payables** | Movement in amounts owed by the business |
| **Change in Inventory** | Movement in stock levels |
| **Change in Other Current Assets** | Movement in other short-term assets |
| **Change in Other Current Liabilities** | Movement in other short-term liabilities |
| **Other Non-Current Assets** | Movement in long-term assets |
| **Other Non-Current Liabilities** | Movement in long-term liabilities |
| **Net Cash from Operations** | Total cash generated from operating and working capital activities |

**Investing Activities**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Property, Plant & Equipment** | Cash spent on or received from fixed assets |
| **Change in Land & Property** | Cash movement related to land and property |
| **Net Cash from Investing** | Total cash used in or generated from investing activities |

**Financing Activities**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Movement in Borrowings** | Cash received from or repaid to lenders |
| **Change in Equity** | Cash from new equity raised or returned to shareholders |
| **Dividends Paid** | Cash distributed to shareholders |
| **Net Cash from Financing** | Total cash generated from or used in financing activities |

**Summary**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Net Cash Flow** | Total net movement in cash for the period |
| **Opening Cash** | Cash balance at the start of the period |
| **Ending Cash** | Cash balance at the end of the period |

## Possible Issues
- Figures not saving — Edit Mode may not be toggled on
- Negative values showing in red — this is expected behaviour indicating a loss or deficit
- Currency showing incorrectly — update the currency using the edit icon next to the currency label

## Possible Remedies
- Toggle **Edit Mode** on before entering or updating figures
- Verify all figures are entered correctly — cross-check against your actual financial records
- Update the currency setting if your financials are in a different currency
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
After completing your financial statements:

1. **Get a Business Valuation** — Your financials feed directly into your valuation report
2. **Upload Financial Model** — Save supporting documents in [My Documents](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/documents)
3. **Complete Investor Readiness** — Strong financials improve your eligibility and preparedness scores
4. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard to track your overall readiness progress
