# Investor Readiness Review (Analyze with Lara AI)

> Get AI-powered insights to accelerate your capital raising journey — completely free.
> Lara AI analyzes your profile to find what is stopping you from getting funded and provides personalized recommendations to close the gaps.
> Access from the Dashboard or Profile page by clicking **Analyze with Lara AI**.

---

## What Lara AI Evaluates
The analysis covers six dimensions of your investor readiness:

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| **Eligibility Score** | Assesses whether your business meets investor criteria |
| **Preparedness Score** | Evaluates how ready you are to raise capital |
| **Risks & Gaps** | Identifies critical issues that could prevent you from getting funded |
| **Business Strengths** | Highlights what makes your business attractive to investors |
| **Document Review** | Critiques your pitch deck and business teaser |
| **Action Plan** | Provides prioritized steps to improve your chances of getting funded |

> Analysis typically completes in **2–3 minutes**. You can continue using the platform while it runs — you will be notified when the report is ready.

---

## Steps

> **Step 1: Start the Analysis**
> Click **Analyze with Lara AI** on the Dashboard or Profile page.
> A modal will open showing the six evaluation dimensions. Click **Start Analysing** to begin.

> **Step 2: Analysis in Progress**
> The modal updates to show *"OUR AI IS EVALUATING"* with a loading indicator on each dimension.
> The button changes to **Analyzing...** — you can close the modal and continue using the platform.

> **Step 3: Report Ready**
> Once complete, all six dimensions show a green checkmark.
> A notification confirms *"Your investor readiness review report is ready."*
> Click **Open Report** to view the full report.

## Readiness Review Report
> Access at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/readiness-review](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/readiness-review)

The report is structured into the following sections:

### Overview
A summary of your overall readiness score and a high-level assessment of your business's strengths and areas for improvement.

### Eligibility vs Preparedness
| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| **Eligibility Score** | How well your business meets investor criteria — based on your Investor Eligibility Assessment |
| **Preparedness Score** | How ready you are to execute a successful capital raise — based on your Investor Preparedness Assessment |
| **Overall Readiness Score** | A combined average of your Eligibility and Preparedness scores |

### Assessment
A narrative interpretation of the gap between your eligibility and preparedness scores, and what it means for your fundraising approach.

### Document Review
An AI review of your uploaded investment documents:
- **Pitch Deck** — Reviewed if uploaded; flagged as *Missing* if not
- **Business Teaser** — Reviewed if uploaded; flagged as *Missing* if not
- **Eligibility Report** — Reviewed based on your completed Investor Eligibility Assessment
- **Preparedness Report** — Reviewed based on your completed Investor Preparedness Assessment

> Each document section includes a **Recommendation** on how to strengthen the document for investor conversations.

### Strengths
A detailed breakdown of what makes your business attractive to investors, with an **Impact** note explaining why each strength matters to investors.
- Examples: *Strong Financial Performance, Experienced Management Team, Robust Intellectual Property, High Customer Satisfaction, Effective Risk Management*

### Risks and Gaps
A detailed list of critical gaps that could prevent your business from getting funded, each with an explanation of why it matters to investors.
- Examples: *Unclear Market Opportunity Data, Undefined Competitive Advantage, Missing Financial Plan, Struggles with Innovation, Past Regulatory Violations*

### Next Steps
A prioritized action plan with specific steps to address each identified gap and improve your overall readiness score.
- Examples: *Conduct Comprehensive Market Analysis, Define Competitive Advantages, Develop Financial Forecasts, Strengthen Regulatory Compliance*

## Possible Issues
- Analysis taking longer than 3 minutes — this may be due to incomplete assessments or a slow connection
- Report shows missing documents — Pitch Deck or Business Teaser have not been uploaded to My Documents
- Low scores on Eligibility or Preparedness — the corresponding assessments have gaps or are incomplete

## Possible Remedies
- Ensure your Investor Eligibility and Investor Preparedness assessments are fully completed before running the analysis
- Upload your Pitch Deck and Business Teaser to [My Documents](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/documents) to get a full document review
- Refresh the page if the analysis appears stuck after 5+ minutes
- If the issue persists, contact our office
