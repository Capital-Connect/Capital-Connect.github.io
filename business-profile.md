# Business Profile

> View and manage your personal and business profile information on Capital Connect Africa.
> Access your profile at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/profile](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/profile)

---

## Sections
- Profile Header
- Investor Readiness Review
- Phone Numbers
- Referral Link
- Business Details
- Mute Capital Raise

---

## Profile Header
Displays your account identity and quick action buttons.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Profile Photo** | Your account avatar or uploaded profile picture |
| **Name** | Your full name as registered on the platform |
| **Email** | Your registered email address |

### Quick Actions

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| **Edit Profile** | Update your personal and business profile information |
| **Your Socials** | Add or manage your social media links |
| **My Referred Users** | View users who signed up using your referral link |

---

## Investor Readiness Review
> Lara AI will analyze your profile to find what is stopping you from getting funded and provide personalized recommendations to close the gaps.

- Click **Analyze with Lara AI** to get a free personalized investor readiness review
- Lara AI identifies gaps in your profile and assessments and recommends specific actions to improve your chances of getting funded

## Phone Numbers
- Displays your registered phone number(s)
- Click **+ Add** to add an additional phone number to your profile

## Referral Link
- A unique referral URL you can share to invite other users to Capital Connect Africa
- Click **Copy** to copy the link to your clipboard
- Example format: `https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth/signup?r=ReferralCode`

## Business Details
A summary of your business profile used for investor matching. These fields are set during Business Profile Setup and can be updated via **Edit Profile**.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Business Name** | The legal or trading name of your business |
| **Country** | The primary country where your business operates |
| **Growth Stage** | Your current business growth stage (e.g. *Growth Stage, Early Stage*) |
| **Funds Needed** | The total investment amount your business is seeking |
| **Employees** | Current number of employees (e.g. *1–10 Employees*) |
| **Years of Operation** | How long the business has been running (e.g. *5–8 years*) |
| **Sector** | The high-level industry your business operates in (e.g. *Food & Agribusiness*) |
| **Sub-Sector** | A more specific category within your sector (e.g. *Food Production*) |
| **Registration Structure** | How your business is legally registered (e.g. *Limited Liability Private Company*) |
| **Segments** | Specific segments within your subsector (e.g. *Crop Farming, Livestock Farming*) |
| **ESG Focus Areas** | The environmental, social, and governance areas your business addresses |
| **Use of Funds** | How the raised investment will be deployed (e.g. *Capital Expenditure, Working Capital*) |
| **Investment Structures** | The types of investment your business is open to receiving |

> The fields marked **required for matching** — Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, and Funds Needed — directly determine which investors are matched to your business. Keeping these accurate and up to date is critical for getting the right investor matches.

## Mute Capital Raise
> Located in the top right of the profile page.

- Toggling this on **hides your business profile** from all investors you are not yet connected with, or those you have connected with and have declined
- Use this when you are not actively fundraising and do not want unsolicited investor visibility
- Toggle it back off when you are ready to be discoverable again

---

## My Invoices
> Accessible from the Profile section in the left sidebar.

- View a history of all payments made on the platform
- Includes invoices for services such as Business Valuation and Advisory Sessions

## Possible Issues
- Profile changes not saving — ensure all required fields are filled before saving
- Referral link not working — verify the link is copied correctly and shared in full
- Business details not reflecting updates — changes made in Edit Profile may take a moment to refresh

## Possible Remedies
- Refresh the page after saving changes to see the latest profile information
- Re-copy the referral link using the **Copy** button to ensure it is complete
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
From the Profile page, the user can navigate to any of the following:

1. **Edit Profile** — Update your business or personal details to improve investor matching
2. **Analyze with Lara AI** — Get a free investor readiness review and personalized recommendations
3. **Share Referral Link** — Invite other businesses or investors to join Capital Connect Africa
4. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard to track your readiness and investor matches
