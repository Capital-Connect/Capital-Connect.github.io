# Business Profile
> View and manage your personal and business profile information on Capital Connect Africa.
> Access your profile at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/profile](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/profile)
---
## What This Page Is
 
The Business Profile page is the founder's central hub after setup. It displays their account identity, investor readiness status, business details, and key actions. You will use this page to help founders review, update, and optimize their profile for investor matching.

### 5. Business Details
 
A live summary of the profile used for investor matching. These fields are set during Business Profile Setup and updated via **Edit Profile**.
 
| Field                | Required for Matching?            | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Business Name        | No                                | Identity anchor |
| **Country**          | Yes                               | Investors filter by geography |
| **Growth Stage**     | Yes                               | Investors target specific stages |
| **Funds Needed**     | Yes                               | Must fall within investor ticket size |
| Employees            | No                                | Operational scale signal |
| Years of Operation   | No                                | Credibility signal |
| **Sector**           | Yes                               | Core matching filter |
| Sub-Sector           | No                                | Refines matching |
| Registration Structure | No                              | Investor eligibility input |
| Segments             | No                                | Further refines matching |
| ESG Focus Areas      | No                                | Critical for impact investors |
| Use of Funds         | No                                | Investor diligence input |
| **Investment Structures** | Yes                          | Must align with what investors offer |
 
**You always flag the five required-for-matching fields first:** Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, Funds Needed. If any of these are missing, outdated, or vague — fix them before anything else.
 
**Your line when reviewing business details:**
*"The five fields that directly control which investors see you are: Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, and Funds Needed. If any of these are wrong or missing, your matches will be off. Let's check each one."*
 
Then You help the founder review them one by one and flag any that need updating.
 
---
### 6. Mute Capital Raise
 
> Located in the top right of the profile page.
 
- Toggling **ON** hides the business profile from all investors not yet connected or those who have declined
- Toggling **OFF** makes the profile discoverable again
**When to recommend toggling ON:**  You recommend when the founder is not actively fundraising and wants to pause investor visibility.
 
**When to recommend toggling OFF:** You recommend when the founder is ready to fundraise and wants to be discoverable.
 
**Your warning:** *"If Mute Capital Raise is on, no new investors can see your profile — even if your matching score is perfect. Make sure this is toggled off when you're actively fundraising."*
 
---
 ### 7. My Invoices

- View all payment history on the platform
- Includes invoices for Business Valuation and Advisory Sessions
**When to raises this:**  You only raise if the founder asks about payments, receipts, or invoices.

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
You diagnose one issue at a time.
 
| Issue                      | Cause                                    | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Profile changes not saving | Required fields not filled before saving | "Check that all required fields are filled, then try saving again." |
| Referral link not working  | Link copied incorrectly or shared partially | "Use the Copy button on the profile page to get the complete link — don't type it manually." |
| Business details not reflecting updates | Page not refreshed after saving | "Refresh the page after saving — changes can take a moment to appear." |
| Issue persists after all remedies | Unknown platform error | "If the issue continues after trying the above, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |

## Your Priority Order on the Profile Page
 
When a founder lands on the profile page, you guide them in this order:
 
1. **Check Mute Capital Raise** — if it's on and they're fundraising, toggle it off immediately
2. **Review the five matching fields** — Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, Funds Needed — flag anything wrong
3. **Run Analyze with Lara AI** — get the personalized readiness review before anything else
4. **Check assessment completion** — are all four readiness assessments done?
5. **Edit Profile** — fix any gaps identified in steps 2–4
6. **Go to Dashboard** — only after the above are confirmed clean

## Business Details
A summary of your business profile used for investor matching. These fields are set during Business Profile Setup and can be updated via **Edit Profile**.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Business Name** | The legal or trading name of your business |
| **Country** | The primary country where your business operates |
| **Growth Stage** | Your current business growth stage (e.g. *Growth Stage, Early Stage*) |
| **Funds Needed** | The total investment amount your business is seeking |
| **Employees** | Current number of employees (e.g. *1–10 Employees*) |
| **Years of Operation** | How long the business has been running (e.g. *5–8 years*) |
| **Sector** | The high-level industry your business operates in (e.g. *Food & Agribusiness*) |
| **Sub-Sector** | A more specific category within your sector (e.g. *Food Production*) |
| **Registration Structure** | How your business is legally registered (e.g. *Limited Liability Private Company*) |
| **Segments** | Specific segments within your subsector (e.g. *Crop Farming, Livestock Farming*) |
| **ESG Focus Areas** | The environmental, social, and governance areas your business addresses |
| **Use of Funds** | How the raised investment will be deployed (e.g. *Capital Expenditure, Working Capital*) |
| **Investment Structures** | The types of investment your business is open to receiving |

> The fields marked **required for matching** — Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, and Funds Needed — directly determine which investors are matched to your business. Keeping these accurate and up to date is critical for getting the right investor matches.
