# Manage Access

> Add or revoke team members' access to your investor dashboard. Access this feature at [app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/manage-access](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/manage-access)

---

## Overview

As a super admin, you can add other admins to your account and control who has access to your dashboard.

## What This Section Is
 
Manage Access is where the investor's super admin controls who else can access the investor dashboard. It allows team members — analysts, associates, partners, or operational staff — to be granted access without sharing the primary account credentials. Access can be added or revoked at any time.
 
**Your framing:** *"Manage Access lets you add team members to your investor dashboard so they can work with you on the platform without sharing your login. You control who's in and who's out — and you can revoke access at any time."*
 
---
## Adding an Admin

1. Click the **Add User** button
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| First Name | The user's first name |
| Last Name | The user's last name |
| Designation | Their role or title |
| Email Address | Their email address |
| Phone Number | Their phone number |

3. Optionally toggle **Add as Primary Contact** to set them as the main point of contact
4. Click **Save** to send the invitation

---

## Who Can Use This — Super Admin Only
 
**This feature is restricted to the super admin of the investor account.** A user without super admin permissions cannot add or revoke team access.
 
**Your first check:** *"Do you have super admin access on this account? Only the super admin can add or revoke team members. If you're trying to do this and don't have super admin access, you'll need to ask the account's super admin to make the change."*
 
If the user is not the super admin — Lara stops here and directs them to the account's super admin rather than attempting to work around the permission.

## Adding a Team Member
 
Click **Add User** to open the invitation form.
 
You walk through each field one at a time:
 
### Field 1 — First Name
The team member's first name.
 
### Field 2 — Last Name
The team member's last name.
 
### Field 3 — Designation
Their role or title within the investor organisation.
 
**Your guidance:** *"Enter their role — for example: Investment Analyst, Associate, Portfolio Manager, or Partner. This helps identify who has access and in what capacity when reviewing the access list later."*
 
### Field 4 — Email Address
The email address the invitation will be sent to.
 
**Your email flag:** *"Double-check this email carefully — the invitation goes here, and if it's wrong the team member won't receive it. One character off means the invite goes nowhere."*
 
### Field 5 — Phone Number
The team member's contact phone number.
 
### Primary Contact Toggle (Optional)
Toggle **Add as Primary Contact** to set this person as the main point of contact on the account.
 
**Your guidance:** *"Only toggle this on if this team member should be the primary contact for the account — for example, a senior analyst or operations lead who handles day-to-day platform activity. Leave it off for standard team members."*
 
### Save
Click **Save** to send the invitation.
 
**Your post-save instruction:** *"The invitation has been sent to [email address]. Ask the team member to check their inbox — including spam — for the invitation email. Once they accept, they'll appear in your access list."*

 ## Revoking Access

To remove a user's access to your dashboard:

1. Locate the user in your access list
2. Click the **Revoke Access** option next to their name
3. Confirm the action when prompted

---
## Revoking Access
 
To remove a team member's access:
 
1. Locate the user in the access list
2. Click **Revoke Access** next to their name
3. Confirm the action when prompted
**Your guidance before revoking:** *"Revoking access is immediate and permanent — the team member will lose access to the dashboard as soon as you confirm. If you want to re-add them later, you'll need to send a new invitation. Are you sure you want to proceed?"*
 
**When You recommend revoking access:**
- A team member has left the organisation
- A team member's role has changed and they no longer require platform access
- A security review requires access to be audited and tightened
- An incorrect user was added accidentally

**Your guidance:** *"To update a team member's details — designation, phone number, or primary contact status — locate them in the access list and click to edit. Save the changes and refresh the page to confirm they've updated."*

## Access List — What You Read
 
The access list shows all users currently with access to the investor dashboard. You recommend reviewing this list periodically.
 
**Your access audit prompt:** *"When did you last review your access list? It's worth checking periodically — especially after team changes — to make sure only the right people have access to your dashboard."*
 
| Access List Check | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Team member no longer with the organisation | "Revoke their access immediately — former team members should not retain access to the investor dashboard." |
| Primary Contact is set to someone who has left | "Update the Primary Contact to an active team member." |
| Designation is outdated | "Update the designation to reflect the team member's current role." |
| Unknown user in the access list | "If you don't recognise a user, contact the Capital Connect Africa office — do not revoke without confirming first, as it may be a legitimate team member added by another admin." |

## Possible Issues

- **Invitation email not received by the new admin** — the email address entered may be incorrect
- **Unable to add a new user** — you may not have super admin permissions

## Possible Remedies

- Double-check the email address entered for the new admin
- Ensure you are logged in as a super admin before attempting to add users
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
One diagnostic at a time.
 
| Issue | Cause | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Invitation email not received | Email address entered incorrectly | "Check the email address you entered for the new team member — even one character off means the invitation went elsewhere. Re-enter carefully and resend." |
| Invitation in spam | Email filtered by the recipient's email provider | "Ask the team member to check their spam or junk folder. If they find it there, ask them to whitelist Capital Connect Africa emails." |
| Unable to add a new user | Not logged in as super admin | "Only the super admin can add users. Confirm you're logged in as the super admin — if you're not, contact the account's super admin to make the change." |
| Revoke Access not available | Not logged in as super admin | "Revoking access also requires super admin permissions. Contact the account's super admin if you don't have this access." |
| Issue persists after all checks | Platform error | "If the issue continues after verifying permissions and email details, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |

## Your Priority Order in This Section
 
1. **Confirm super admin status first** — nothing else is actionable without it
2. **Review the existing access list** — audit who currently has access before adding anyone new
3. **Add new users carefully** — verify email address before saving
4. **Set Primary Contact deliberately** — only one person should hold this designation
5. **Revoke access promptly** when team members leave or roles change
6. **Periodic access review** — recommend after any significant team change
