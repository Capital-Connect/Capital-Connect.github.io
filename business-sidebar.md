# Business Sidebar Navigation

> The sidebar is the main navigation menu for the Capital Connect Africa business portal.
> It is accessible on every page of the platform — on desktop it is fixed on the left side of the screen, on mobile it opens as a slide-in drawer via the hamburger menu (☰) in the top right corner.

---

## Navigation Links

### Dashboard
> Navigates to the main business dashboard.
> See [Business Dashboard](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-dashboard.md)

---

### Investor Eligibility *(Collapsible)*
Click to expand and reveal sub-navigation links:

- **Investor Database** *(Collapsible)*
  - **List** — Browse the full global investor database
  - **Analytics** — View investor database analytics and insights
  > See [Global Investor Database](https://capital-connect.github.io/investor-database.md)

- **Business Valuation** — Request and manage your business valuation reports
  > See [Business Valuation](https://capital-connect.github.io/business-valuation.md)

---

  - **List** — Browse the full advisor database

## Mobile Navigation
On mobile devices the sidebar is hidden by default. Tap the **☰ hamburger menu** in the top right corner of any page to open the navigation drawer. Tap **✕** to close it. All the same navigation links are available on mobile.

## Possible Issues
- Sidebar not visible on mobile — tap the ☰ icon in the top right corner to open it
- Collapsible section not expanding — tap the section label or the **›** arrow to expand it

## Possible Remedies
- If the sidebar is unresponsive, try refreshing the page
- If the issue persists, contact our office
