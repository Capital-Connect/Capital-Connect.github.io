# Login

> Login to Capital Connect Africa Portal

## Requirements
- Email Address
- Password

> Step 1: Open [The Login Page](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth)
> Step 2: Enter your email and password and click on the login button

## Possible Issues
- "Invalid credentials. Please check your password or create an account if you don't have one" Error - for incorrect password or email

## Possible Remedies
- User to verify that they provided a valid email.
- User to check that they provided a valid password.
- User to reset the password instead if they forgot their password.
- If all the above check, then contact our office 

## Next Steps
On successful login some or all of these could be the next steps 
1. **Verify Email**: When logging in for the first time or did not verify before
2. **Create Profile**: When it's first time logging in or did not complete the process before
3. **Explore Platform**: Explore the on successful login
