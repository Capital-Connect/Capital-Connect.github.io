# Business Dashboard

> Your central point for tracking investor readiness, viewing matched investors, and accessing your investment assets.
> Access the dashboard at [https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business)

---


## Sections
- Readiness Score Carousel
- Profile Optimization
- Investor Matches Breakdown
- Investor Readiness Reports
- Call to Actions (Get Valuation, Book Advisory Session, Investor Readiness Review)

---

## Readiness Score Carousel
Displays your investor readiness scores across three views — toggle between tabs to see each score:

- **Overall** — The combined average of your Eligibility and Preparedness scores
- **Eligibility** — Your score from the Investor Eligibility Assessment
- **Readiness** — Your score from the Investor Preparedness Assessment

> Investors prioritize profiles with scores above 80%. A prompt is shown when your score falls below this threshold, indicating which score needs improvement.

---

## Profile Optimization
Highlights specific actions you can take to improve your profile and increase your readiness score.

- Displayed as a list of actionable items directly below the readiness score carousel
- Each item points to a specific gap in your profile or assessments
- Resolving these items improves your overall score and increases visibility to investors

> Example: *"Improve Preparedness Score"* — indicates the Investor Preparedness Assessment is incomplete or has gaps.

---

## Investor Matches Breakdown
Shows the total number of investors matched to your business profile based on your matching criteria.

| Metric | Description | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Platform Matches** | Investors on the Capital Connect platform matched to your profile | Click to see a pop-up list of matched platform investors |
| **Inbound Requests** | Connection requests received from investors who have expressed interest in your business | Click to see a list of investors who want to connect |
| **Global Prospects** | Investors matched from the global investor database beyond the platform | Click to explore the full global investor database |

> Matching is driven by your Business Profile — specifically your Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, and Funds Needed. Incomplete profiles return fewer matches.

---

## Investor Readiness Reports
Auto-generated reports based on your completed assessments. These are the investment assets investors can review when they visit your profile.

| Report | Description | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Eligibility Report** | Proves your business fits the investor mandate. Scored out of 100% | Shown with score (e.g. *Score: 88%*) once Eligibility Assessment is complete |
| **Readiness Report** | Identifies gaps in your business before investors do. Scored out of 100% | Shown with score (e.g. *Score: 66%*) once Preparedness Assessment is complete |
| **Impact Assessment** | Quantifies your business's social and ESG value | Shows *Ready* once Impact Assessment is complete |
| **Business Fact Sheet** | A one-page investor snapshot of your business | Shows *Ready* once Business Factsheet is complete |

> All four reports are auto-generated — no manual formatting needed. Complete the corresponding assessment to unlock each report.

## Possible Issues
- Low readiness score — one or more assessments are incomplete or have gaps
- No investor matches — required matching fields in the Business Profile are incomplete
- Reports not showing — corresponding assessment has not been completed yet

## Possible Remedies
- Complete all four assessments to unlock all reports and improve your score
- Ensure all five matching fields in your Business Profile are filled (Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, Funds Needed)
- Use the Profile Optimization section on the dashboard to identify and resolve specific gaps
- If the issue persists, contact our office
