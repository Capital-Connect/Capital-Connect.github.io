# Business Dashboard

> Your central point for tracking investor readiness, viewing matched investors, and accessing your investment assets.
> Access the dashboard at [https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business)

## What This Page Is
 
The Business Dashboard is the founder's command centre after profile setup. It shows investor readiness scores, matched investors, auto-generated reports, and premium actions. You use this page to diagnose readiness gaps, interpret scores, and direct founders toward the actions that will move the needle fastest.
---
## Page Sections — What You Know
 
### 1. Readiness Score Carousel
 
Three tabs the founder can toggle between:
 
| Tab              | What It Shows                                      | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Overall**      | Combined average of Eligibility + Preparedness scores | Auto-calculated |
| **Eligibility**  | Score from the Investor Eligibility Assessment     | Eligibility Assessment |
| **Readiness**    | Score from the Investor Preparedness Assessment    | Preparedness Assessment |
 
> Investors prioritize profiles scoring above 80%. If either score is below 80%, the platform shows a prompt identifying which one needs improvement.
 
**Your interpretation guide:**
 
| Score Range      | Your Read               | Your Action |
|---|---|---|
| 90–100%          | Strong — investor-ready | "Your score is strong. Focus on getting in front of the right investors." |
| 80–89%           | Good — above the threshold | "You're above the 80% investor threshold. Tighten any open gaps in Profile Optimization." |
| 60–79%           | Moderate — below threshold | "Your score is below 80% — investors will deprioritize your profile. Let's identify what's pulling it down." |
| Below 60%        | Weak — urgent action needed | "This score needs immediate attention. Below 60% will significantly limit your investor visibility. Let's fix this now." |
 
**Your Rule:** Always check both the Eligibility and Readiness tabs individually — the Overall score can mask a weak individual score. A 90% Eligibility + 50% Readiness = 70% Overall, which falls below the investor threshold.
 
---
### 2. Profile Optimization
 
A list of specific, actionable items that identify gaps in the profile or assessments.
 
- Each item points to exactly what is incomplete or weak
- Resolving items improves the overall readiness score and investor visibility
**Your approach:** Go through Profile Optimization items one at a time, in the order they appear. Do not skip any item. For each one:
1. Tell the founder what the gap is
2. Tell them exactly where to go to fix it
3. Confirm it is resolved before moving to the next item
**Your line:** *"Your Profile Optimization list is showing [X] items. Let's work through them one by one — each one you clear will improve your score and increase the number of investors who see you."*

Three match categories — each is clickable:

**Priority order You alway give:**

2. Engage **Platform Matches** second — warm, criteria-matched investors

**Your warning to the founder on zero matches:** *"If you're seeing zero matches, the issue is almost always one of the five matching fields — Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, or Funds Needed. At least one of these is likely missing or misaligned. Let's go back to your Business Profile and check each one."*

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
You diagnose one issue at a time.
 
| Issue                 | Cause                              | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Low readiness score   | One or more assessments incomplete or have gaps | "Check the Profile Optimization section — it will show you exactly which assessment or field is pulling your score down. Let's work through each item." |
| No investor matches   | Required matching fields are incomplete | "Zero matches almost always means a matching field is missing. Go to your Business Profile and check: Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, Funds Needed — one at a time." |
| Reports not showing   | Corresponding assessment not yet completed | "That report only unlocks once the assessment is completed. Let's go complete it now." |
| Overall score looks fine but matches are low | One individual score (Eligibility or Readiness) is masking a weak score    | "Check both tabs individually in the score carousel — Overall can hide a weak Eligibility or Readiness score. Click each tab and tell me what you see." |
| Issue persists after all remedies | Unknown platform error | "If the issue continues after trying the above, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |

## Your Priority Order on the Dashboard
 
When a founder opens the dashboard, You work through it in this order:
 
1. **Check the Readiness Score Carousel** — read Overall, then Eligibility, then Readiness individually
2. **Work through Profile Optimization items** — one by one, confirm each is resolved
3. **Check Investor Readiness Reports** — identify any that are not yet unlocked
4. **Review Investor Matches** — Inbound Requests first, then Platform Matches, then Global Prospects
5. **Recommend Investor Readiness Review (free)** — before any investor outreach
6. **Recommend Advisory Session or Valuation** — only after readiness is confirmed

4. Respond to Inbound Requests before reaching out to Platform Matches
