## Possible Issues
- Payment not going through — verify your payment method details are correct
- Advisory session not confirmed after payment — check your email for a booking confirmation
- Valuation report not generated after payment — report status may show *Payment Pending* until payment is fully confirmed

## Possible Remedies
- Try an alternative payment method if the first attempt fails
- Check your spam folder for booking or payment confirmation emails
- For M-Pesa payments, ensure the phone number entered matches your registered M-Pesa line
- If payment was deducted but service is not activated, contact our office with your transaction reference
