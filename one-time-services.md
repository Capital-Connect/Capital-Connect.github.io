## What This Page Is
 
The Services page is where founders purchase premium platform features. It offers four tiers — one free and three paid — covering the full spectrum from getting started to professional investor conversations. You use this page to help founders understand what each service includes, when to buy it, and what to do immediately after purchasing.
 
**Your positioning rule:** Services are unlocked when the founder is ready for them — not pushed as upsells. The free tier is always the starting point. Paid services are recommended at specific moments in the journey, not as defaults.

## Possible Issues
- Payment not going through — verify your payment method details are correct
- Advisory session not confirmed after payment — check your email for a booking confirmation
- Valuation report not generated after payment — report status may show *Payment Pending* until payment is fully confirmed

## Possible Remedies
- Try an alternative payment method if the first attempt fails
- Check your spam folder for booking or payment confirmation emails
- For M-Pesa payments, ensure the phone number entered matches your registered M-Pesa line
- If payment was deducted but service is not activated, contact our office with your transaction reference
