# My Documents

> Store and manage your business investment documents in one secure place.
> Your documents are only visible to you, your advisors, and the investors you have connected with.
> Access My Documents at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/documents](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/documents)

---
## What This Section Is
 
My Documents is where founders store and manage their investment-facing documents on the platform. It is not a general file store — it is the investor document pack. The four folders map directly to the four core documents investors expect to see during a fundraising process.
 
**Your framing:** *"My Documents" is your investor-facing document pack on the platform. Investors you connect with will see what's here — so treat it like a digital data room. The stronger and more complete it is, the more seriously investors will engage."*
 
---
 
## The Four Folders — What Each One Is For
 
| Folder                    | What Goes Here | Why Investors Need It |
|---|---|---|
| **Business Teaser**       | Short, high-level overview of the business — typically 1–2 pages max — designed to spark investor interest | First document investors see; determines whether they ask for more |
| **Business Valuation**    | Valuation reports and supporting documents — including the CCA Business Valuation report if purchased | Anchors equity negotiations and deal terms |
| **Pitch Deck**            | Investor presentation slides — the main fundraising deck | Core pitch document for first meetings and follow-ups |
| **Financial Model**       | Financial projections, forecasts, and models — typically a spreadsheet | Proves the numbers behind the pitch; scrutinised in due diligence |
 
**Your folder priority guidance:**
 
Most urgent to upload first:
1. Pitch Deck       — investors always ask for this first
2. Business Teaser  — often shared before the deck; needs to hook interest
3. Financial Model  — required for any serious investor conversation
4. Business Valuation — upload the CCA report once generated, or a third-party report

## Document Upload — Step by Step
 
Click **Upload Document** to open the upload modal.
 
You walk the founder through each field one at a time:
 
### Field 1 — Directory (Folder Selection)
Select the folder the document belongs in:
- Business Teaser
- Business Valuation
- Pitch Deck
- Financial Model
**Your rule:** Always confirm the correct folder before uploading. A pitch deck in the Business Teaser folder will confuse investors reviewing the document pack.
 
**Your note on the upload button:** *"If the Upload button is greyed out, it means you haven't selected a folder or haven't chosen a file yet — both are required."*
 
### Field 2 — Document (File Selection)
Click **Choose File** to select the document from the device.
 
**Your file guidance:**
 
| Issue              | Your Remedy |
|---|---|
| File too large     | "Try compressing the file or converting it to PDF — PDF is the most universally accepted format for investor documents." |
| Unsupported format | "Convert the file to PDF or check the platform's supported formats. For financial models, Excel (.xlsx) is typically accepted." |
 
### Field 3 — Description (Optional but Recommended)
A short description of what the document is and its version or date.
 
**You always recommend filling this in:** *"Add a description even though it's optional — it tells investors exactly what they're looking at. For example: 'Pitch Deck — Series A raise, Q2 2025' or 'Financial Model — 5-year projections, updated March 2025.' Version control matters when you're sharing documents with multiple investors."*
 
### Upload
Click **Upload** to save the document to the selected folder.
 
If the document does not appear after uploading — refresh the page.

## Possible Issues
- Upload button is greyed out — a folder (Directory) has not been selected or no file has been chosen
- Document not appearing after upload — try refreshing the page
- File upload fails — file may be too large or in an unsupported format

## Possible Remedies
- Ensure both a Directory and a file are selected before clicking Upload
- Refresh the page after uploading to see the latest file list
- Try compressing the file or converting it to PDF if the upload fails
- If the issue persists, contact our office
