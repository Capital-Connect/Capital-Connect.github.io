# My Invoices

> Keep track of all payments you have made on the Capital Connect Africa platform.
> Access My Invoices at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/invoices](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/invoices)

---

## Sections
- Overview
- Invoice History
- Invoice Details

---

## Overview
Four summary cards at the top of the page showing your overall billing status at a glance:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Billed** | The total amount invoiced to your account across all transactions |
| **Amount Paid** | The total amount successfully paid |
| **Pending Payment** | The total amount invoiced but not yet paid |
| **Payment Failed** | The total amount where payment attempts were unsuccessful |

---

## Invoice History
A paginated table listing all invoices on your account, sorted by most recent first.

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Amount Billed** | The amount charged for the specific transaction |
| **Date Invoiced** | The date the invoice was issued |
| **Status** | The current payment status of the invoice |
| **Actions** | **Open** to view the full invoice details; **Download** to save a copy |

### Invoice Status Labels

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fulfilled** | Payment was successfully completed |
| **Pending** | Payment has been initiated but not yet confirmed |
| **Payment Failed** | Payment attempt was unsuccessful |

---

## Invoice Details
Click **Open** on any invoice row to view the full invoice in a modal popup.

Each invoice shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **From** | Capital Connect (the issuing entity) |
| **Invoice Number** | A unique identifier for the invoice (e.g. *#1857*) |
| **Issue Date** | The date the invoice was created |
| **Due Date** | The date payment was due |
| **Status** | Current payment status (e.g. *Fulfilled, Pending*) |
| **Bill To** | Your business name and the name of the account holder who initiated the payment |
| **Description** | The service the payment was for (e.g. *Business Valuation Payment*) |
| **Amount Billed** | The gross amount charged |
| **Discount** | Any discount applied to the invoice |
| **Total Paid** | The final amount paid after discounts |

> Click **Download** within the invoice modal to save a copy of the invoice to your device.

## Possible Issues
- Invoice status shows *Pending* for an extended period — payment may not have been confirmed by the payment provider
- Invoice status shows *Payment Failed* — payment was not successfully processed
- Invoice not appearing in history — try refreshing the page

## Possible Remedies
- For *Pending* invoices, wait a few minutes and refresh — M-Pesa and mobile money payments can take a few minutes to confirm
- For *Payment Failed* invoices, retry the payment via the relevant service page (e.g. Business Valuation or Services)
- If a payment was deducted from your account but the invoice still shows *Pending* or *Failed*, contact our office with your transaction reference number

## Next Steps
From My Invoices, the user can navigate to:

1. **Download an Invoice** — Save a copy of any invoice for your records
2. **Retry a Failed Payment** — Head to [Services](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/subscriptions/business) or [Business Valuation](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/valuations) to retry
3. **Go to Profile** — Return to your general profile page
4. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard
