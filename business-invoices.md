# My Invoices

> Keep track of all payments you have made on the Capital Connect Africa platform.
> Access My Invoices at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/invoices](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/invoices)

## What This Section Is
 
My Invoices is the billing history for all payments made on the Capital Connect Africa platform. It tracks invoices for services like Business Valuation and Advisory Sessions, shows payment status, and allows the founder to download copies for their records.
 
**You use this section when:**
- A founder asks about a payment they made
- A service is showing as unpurchased or inaccessible despite payment
- A founder needs an invoice for accounting or tax purposes
- A payment has failed or been pending for too long
---
 
## Overview Cards — What You Reads First
 
Four summary cards at the top of the page give an immediate billing status snapshot:
 
| Card                | What It Shows | Your Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Total Billed**    | Total amount invoiced across all transactions | Context for the full billing history |
| **Amount Paid**     | Total successfully paid | Should match sum of all Fulfilled invoices |
| **Pending Payment** | Amount invoiced but not yet confirmed | Flag if non-zero — a pending payment may mean a service not yet activated |
| **Payment Failed**  | Amount where payment attempts failed | Flag immediately — failed payments need to be retried |
 
**Your immediate priority:** If **Pending Payment** or **Payment Failed** is non-zero, address those before anything else.
 
*"You have a pending or failed payment showing on your account. Let's resolve that first before we look at anything else — a failed payment means the service hasn't been activated."*

## Invoice History Table
 
A paginated list of all invoices, sorted most recent first.
 
| Column            | What You Read |
|---|---|
| **Amount Billed** | The charge for that specific transaction |
| **Date Invoiced** | When the invoice was issued — recency matters for pending items |
| **Status**        | Current payment state — see status labels below |
| **Actions**       | Open (view full details) or Download (save a copy) |
 
### Invoice Status Labels — Your Read
 
| Status            | What It Means | Your Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Fulfilled**     | Payment successfully completed | Service is active. "Download this invoice if you need it for your records." |
| **Pending**       | Payment initiated but not yet confirmed | "Wait a few minutes and refresh — M-Pesa and mobile money payments can take a few minutes to confirm. If it's been more than 10 minutes, check with your payment provider or contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |
| **Payment Failed** | Payment attempt was unsuccessful | "This payment didn't go through — the service won't be activated until it's retried successfully. Go to [Services](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/subscriptions/business) or [Business Valuation](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/valuations) to retry the payment." |

## Invoice Details — What You Know
 
Click **Open** on any invoice row to view the full invoice in a modal popup.
 
| Field              | What You Check |
|---|---|
| **From**           | Capital Connect — confirms the issuing entity |
| **Invoice Number** | Unique identifier — needed when contacting the office about a payment issue |
| **Issue Date**     | When the invoice was created |
| **Due Date**       | When payment was due |
| **Status**         | Current state — Fulfilled, Pending, or Payment Failed |
| **Bill To**        | Business name and account holder — confirms the invoice is for the correct account |
| **Description**    | The service paid for — e.g. *Business Valuation Payment*, *Advisory Session* |
| **Amount Billed**  | Gross charge |
| **Discount**       | Any voucher or discount applied |
| **Total Paid**     | Final amount after discounts |
 
**Your invoice reference rule:** *"When contacting the Capital Connect Africa office about a payment issue, always have the Invoice Number ready — it's the unique identifier they'll use to locate your transaction. You can find it by clicking Open on the relevant invoice."*

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
One diagnostic at a time.
 
| Issue                                              | Cause         | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Invoice status shows *Pending* for extended period | Payment not yet confirmed by provider | "Wait a few minutes and refresh the page — mobile money payments like M-Pesa can take a few minutes to confirm. If it's been more than 10 minutes, contact your payment provider first." |
| Invoice status shows *Payment Failed*              | Payment not successfully processed | "Go to the relevant service page to retry: Services at https://app.capitalconnect.africa/subscriptions/business or Business Valuation at https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/valuations." |
| Invoice not appearing in history                   | Page not refreshed after payment | "Refresh the page — newly issued invoices can take a moment to appear." |
| Payment was deducted but invoice still shows Pending or Failed | Transaction completed on the payment side but not confirmed on the platform       | "Don't retry the payment yet — contact the Capital Connect Africa office with your transaction reference number. They can verify the deduction and activate the service manually." |
 
**Critical rule on double payments:** If money has already been deducted from the founder's account, You tell them to contact the office *before* retrying — to avoid a duplicate charge.

## Downloading an Invoice
 
Click **Download** on any invoice row in the history table, or within the invoice modal, to save a copy.
 
**When You recommend downloading:**
- Founder needs the invoice for business accounting records
- Founder is requesting reimbursement for a platform expense
- Tax or audit purposes require documented proof of payment
*"Click Download on the invoice — it saves a copy directly to your device. If you need it for accounting or tax, download it now and file it with your records."*
 
---
 
## Your Priority Order in This Section
1. **Check Pending Payment and Payment Failed cards first** — non-zero values need immediate attention
2. **For Payment Failed — route to retry immediately** — Services or Business Valuation page
3. **For Pending (10+ minutes) — advise contacting payment provider or office** — do not retry
4. **If money deducted but status wrong — contact office with transaction reference** — do not retry
5. **For Fulfilled invoices — download if needed** — otherwise no action required
---
 
## You Next Steps: Always Deliver
1. If Payment Failed — go to Services or Business Valuation to retry the payment
2. If Pending (10+ minutes) — contact payment provider or the Capital Connect Africa office
3. If money deducted but wrong status — contact office with Invoice Number and transaction reference before retrying
4. Download any invoices needed for accounting or tax records
5. Return to the Dashboard to continue the fundraising journey

Each invoice shows:

| Field | Description |

| **Discount** | Any discount applied to the invoice |
| **Total Paid** | The final amount paid after discounts |

## Possible Issues
- Invoice status shows *Pending* for an extended period — payment may not have been confirmed by the payment provider
- Invoice status shows *Payment Failed* — payment was not successfully processed
- Invoice not appearing in history — try refreshing the page

## Possible Remedies
- For *Pending* invoices, wait a few minutes and refresh — M-Pesa and mobile money payments can take a few minutes to confirm
- For *Payment Failed* invoices, retry the payment via the relevant service page (e.g. Business Valuation or Services)
- If a payment was deducted from your account but the invoice still shows *Pending* or *Failed*, contact our office with your transaction reference number

## Next Steps
From My Invoices, the user can navigate to:

1. **Download an Invoice** — Save a copy of any invoice for your records
2. **Retry a Failed Payment** — Head to [Services](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/subscriptions/business) or [Business Valuation](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/valuations) to retry
3. **Go to Profile** — Return to your general profile page
4. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard
