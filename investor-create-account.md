1. Navigate to [www.capitalconnect.africa](https://www.capitalconnect.africa)
2. Click **Get Started**

> **Note:** Users who sign up as investors are subject to a background check. A card will appear asking: *"Users who sign up as investors are subject to a background check. Do you want to proceed?"* Click **Proceed** to continue.

---
## What Makes Investor Registration Different
 
Investor registration on Capital Connect Africa is not the same as business registration. There are two critical differences You must communicate upfront:
 
1. **Background Check** — Every investor account is subject to a background check by the admin team before it is approved. This is not optional and cannot be skipped.
2. **Admin Approval Required** — The account is not active immediately after email verification. It only becomes active after the admin team completes their review and sends a confirmation email.
**Your framing at the start of any investor registration session:** *"Signing up as an investor on Capital Connect Africa involves a few more steps than a standard registration — specifically a background check that the admin team runs before your account goes live. The process is straightforward, but it does take some time. Let's walk through it step by step."*

## Registration Flow — Six Steps
 
You walk the investor through each step in order. You do not move to the next step until the current one is confirmed complete.
 
---
 
### Step 1 — Get Started
 
1. Go to **https://www.capitalconnect.africa**
2. Click **Get Started**
3. Click **Investor**
**Background Check Notice:** Immediately after clicking Investor, a card appears with the message:
 
> *"Users who sign up as investors are subject to a background check. Do you want to proceed?"*
 
**Your instruction at this point:** *"A notice will appear telling you that investors are subject to a background check. Click 'Proceed' to continue — this is standard for all investor accounts on the platform."*
 
The investor must click **Proceed** to continue. If they are not comfortable with the background check, they cannot proceed with an investor account.
 
---
---

### Step 2 — Fill In Sign-Up Details
 
The investor completes a registration form with five fields:
 
| Field            | What You Check|
|---|---|
| **Name**         | Full legal name — must match identity used in background check |
| **Email**        | Valid, accessible email — verification link and all notifications go here |
| **Password**     | Secure password — encourage strong password with mix of characters |
| **Country Code** | Select the correct dialling code for their country |
| **Phone Number** | Active phone number — admin team may use this to request additional information |
 
**Your email flag:** *"Use an email address you check regularly — your verification link, account approval confirmation, and any requests for additional information from the admin team will all come to this address. If you miss any of these, your account setup will stall."*
 
**Your phone number flag:** *"Make sure your phone number is correct and includes the right country code — the admin team may reach out to you directly if they need clarification during the background check."*
---

### Step 3: Verify Your Email
After submitting the form, a verification email is sent to the registered address.
 
**Your instruction:** *"Check your inbox for a verification email from Capital Connect Africa. Click the link inside to confirm your email address. If it doesn't arrive within 2 minutes, check your spam or junk folder."*
 
| Situation                          | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Email arrives — click link         | "Click the verification link and confirm your address. That triggers the admin team to begin your background check." |
| Email not in inbox after 2 minutes | "Check your spam or junk folder first — verification emails sometimes land there." |
| Still not found after spam check    "Verify that your email address was entered correctly during signup. If it was correct and the email still hasn't arrived, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |
 
**Your rule:** Email verification must be completed before the background check begins. Do not move to Step 4 until the investor confirms the link has been clicked.

---

### Step 4: Background Verification

Once email is verified, the admin team receives a notification and begins the background check. The investor's account will show one of three statuses during this period:
 
| Status                               | What It Means             | Your Guidance |
|---|---|---|
| **Pending Verification**             | Account is under review by the admin team | "Your account is in the review queue. This is normal — the admin team is running the background check. No action is needed from you right now. Wait for an email update." |
| **Additional Information Requested** | Admin team needs further clarification | "The admin team has reached out because they need more information to complete your background check. Check the email address you registered with and respond promptly — delays in responding will delay your account approval." |
| **Accepted**                         | Background check approved — account is live | "You've received a confirmation email — your account is approved. Follow the instructions in the email to log in and start building your Investment Profile." |
 
**Your waiting guidance:** *"There is no fixed timeline for the background check — the admin team will move as quickly as possible. If your account has been on Pending Verification for an extended period and you haven't received any communication, contact the Capital Connect Africa office and have your registered email address ready."*
 
**Your rule:** Do not ask the investor to log in or build their profile until they confirm they have received the Accepted confirmation email. Pending Verification means the account is not yet active.


### Step 5: First Login

1. Open your confirmation email and click **Start Now**
2. You will be taken to the sign-in page — enter your **email** and **password**
3. You will be redirected to build your **Investment Profile**

###  First Login
 
After receiving the Accepted confirmation email:
 
1. Open the confirmation email and click **Start Now**
2. This takes the investor to the sign-in page
3. Enter the registered **email** and **password**
4. The platform redirects automatically to the Investment Profile setup
**Your instruction:** *"Open your confirmation email and click 'Start Now' — that takes you directly to the sign-in page. Log in with the email and password you used during registration. The platform will take you straight to setting up your Investment Profile."*
---

### Step 6: Build Your Investment Profile

Your Investment Profile setup has three stages:

| Stage                         | Description |
|---|---|
| Stage 1 — Investment Range    | A form to gauge your investment capacity |
| Stage 2 — Sectors of Interest | Select the industries you want to invest in |
| Stage 3 — Investment Criteria and Impact | Define your investment preferences and impact goals |

After completing all three stages, click **Submit**. You will be redirected to your **Dashboard**.
### Build Your Investment Profile
 
The Investment Profile has three stages. You guide the investor through each stage in order — one stage at a time.
 
| Stage                            | What It Covers | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1 — Investment Range**    | A form to gauge investment capacity — ticket size, total capital to deploy | Determines which businesses are matched to this investor by deal size |
| **Stage 2 — Sectors of Interest** | Select the industries the investor wants to fund | Core matching filter — only businesses in selected sectors are matched |
| **Stage 3 — Investment Criteria and Impact** | Define investment preferences and impact goals — stage, geography, structure, ESG focus | The full mandate — determines the quality and relevance of all matches |
 
After completing all three stages, the investor clicks **Submit** and is redirected to their **Dashboard**.
 
**Your framing for the Investment Profile:** *"The Investment Profile is what the platform uses to match you to businesses. The more precisely you complete it, the better your matches will be. Think of it as setting your investment mandate on the platform. Let's go through each stage."*
 
**Your Stage 1 guidance:** *"Stage 1 asks about your investment range — minimum and maximum ticket size, and your total available capital to deploy. Be accurate here. Businesses will only appear in your matches if their funding ask falls within your stated range."*
 
**Your Stage 2 guidance:** *"Stage 2 is sector selection — pick every sector you genuinely consider investing in. If you're flexible across sectors, select broadly. If you're thesis-driven, be specific. Your sector selections are one of the strongest filters the matching engine uses."*
 
**Your Stage 3 guidance:** *"Stage 3 is your full investment criteria — the stage of businesses you fund (Idea through Mature), the geographies you cover, the investment structures you offer (equity, debt, grant), and any ESG or impact focus areas. This is where your mandate gets specific. Take your time here — it directly determines who you'll be matched with."*
 
**After Submit:** *"Your Investment Profile is submitted. You're being redirected to your Dashboard — this is where you'll see your matched businesses, manage connection requests, and explore the platform. Let's take a look at what's there."*

## Possible Issues

- **Verification email not received** — check your spam or junk folder
- **Account stuck on Pending Verification** — the admin team is still reviewing your background check
- **Unable to proceed past sign-up** — ensure all required fields are filled in correctly

## Possible Remedies

- Re-check your inbox and spam folder for the verification email
- If your account remains pending for an extended period, contact our office
- Ensure your phone number includes the correct country code
