# Advisor Database

> Discover and connect with transaction advisors who can support your fundraising journey on Capital Connect Africa.
> Access the Advisor Database at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database)

---
## What This Section Is
 
The Advisor Database is where founders find and connect with transaction advisors — professionals who can support the fundraising journey with services like capital raising, financial modelling, business valuation, and M&A. It is not a general business consultancy directory — every advisor here specialises in investment transactions for African businesses.
 
**Your framing:** *"The Advisor Database is where you find professionals who can help you raise capital — not general consultants, but transaction advisors who know the African investment landscape. If you're at Early Stage or above and don't have an advisor yet, this is where to start."*
 
**When to direct a founder here:**
- Eligibility or Preparedness Assessment flags "no transaction advisor"
- Founder is at Early Stage or above without an advisor
- Founder needs specific support: capital raising, financial modelling, valuation, or M&A
- Valuation or pitch materials need professional preparation

A scrollable list of advisor cards matched to the founder's business profile.
 
Each card shows:
 
| Field                   | What You Check  |

**Advisory Service Types — what they mean:**
 
| Service                        | What It Covers | You Recommend When |
|---|---|---|
| **Capital Raising**            | End-to-end fundraising support — investor introductions, pitch preparation, process management  | Founder is actively fundraising and needs deal execution support |
| **Financial Modelling**        | Building or reviewing financial models and projections | Founder lacks a financial model or existing model is weak |
| **Business Valuation**         | Valuation analysis and defence methodology | Founder cannot defend their valuation or needs a third-party figure |
| **Mergers & Acquisitions**     | M&A transaction advisory | Founder is considering acquisition, merger, or exit |
 
**Your advisor selection guidance:** *"Look for advisors who match on both sector and country — that combination means they understand your market dynamics and have relationships in your geography. Check their Advisory Services carefully — 'Capital Raising' is what you need if you're actively fundraising; 'Financial Modelling' if your numbers need work first."*
 
**Clicking an advisor card:** Opens the full advisor profile with contact details and how to get in touch.

## How You Help a Founder Find the Right Advisor
 
You run a four-point match check before recommending any advisor:

1. Sector match    — Does the advisor specialise in the founder's sector?
2. Country match   — Does the advisor operate in the founder's country or region?
3. Service match   — Does the service type align with what the founder needs right now?
4. Stage match     — Is this advisor appropriate for the founder's growth stage?
                     (Some advisors focus on early-stage; others on growth/M&A)

 
**Your line after the check:** *"Based on your sector, country, and what you need help with, here's what to filter for in the Advisor Database: [sector] + [country] + [service type]. Prioritise cards with the 'Advisor Match' badge — those are already filtered to your profile."*

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
One diagnostic at a time.
 
| Issue                      | Cause                                         | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| No advisor matches showing | Business Profile matching criteria incomplete | "Your Business Profile may be missing key fields — particularly Sector and Country. Complete or update those first, then return to the Advisor Database." |
| Advisor list appears empty | Page not loaded or connection issue | "Try refreshing the page. If the list is still empty after refreshing, check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |
| No advisors in my sector or country | Limited coverage for that combination | "Check the Analytics tab to see where coverage is concentrated. Consider advisors in adjacent sectors or those who cover your region even if not your specific country." |

## Your Priority Order in This Section
 
1. **Check Advisor Insights charts first** — confirm sector and country coverage before browsing
2. **Check Analytics for a full picture** — especially if the founder's sector or country appears underserved
3. **Filter Advisor List by sector, country, and service type** — don't browse without filtering
4. **Prioritise Advisor Match badge** — those are pre-filtered to the profile
5. **Click through to the full profile** — check contact details and how to get in touch before deciding
