# Advisor Database

> Discover and connect with transaction advisors who can support your fundraising journey on Capital Connect Africa.
> Access the Advisor Database at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database)

---

## Sections
- Advisor Insights (charts)
- Advisor List
- Advisor Analytics

---

## Advisor Insights
Two bar charts giving a quick visual overview of the advisor landscape:

| Chart | Description |
|---|---|
| **Advisors by Sector** | Distribution of advisors across sectors they specialize in — e.g. Finance, Energy, Agriculture, Real Estate, Health |
| **Advisors by Country** | Distribution of advisors by the countries they operate in — e.g. Kenya, Ghana, Uganda, Nigeria, Somalia, Tanzania |

---

## Advisor List
A scrollable list of advisor cards matched to your business profile. Each card shows:

- **Advisor Match badge** — Confirms this advisor is a match for your business
- **Advisor Name** — The name of the advisory firm or individual
- **Sectors** — The sectors the advisor specializes in
- **Countries** — The African countries the advisor operates in
- **Advisory Services** — The types of advisory services offered
  > Examples: *Capital Raising, Financial Modeling, Business Valuation, Mergers & Acquisitions*

> Click on any advisor card to view their full profile and get in touch.

---

## Advisor Analytics
A detailed analytics view of the full advisor landscape on the platform.
> Access via the **Analytics** tab under Advisor Database in the left sidebar.
> URL: [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database/analytics](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/advisors-database/analytics)

### Overview Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Advisors** | Total number of advisors in the database |
| **Top Country** | The country with the highest concentration of advisors |
| **Top Sector** | The sector attracting the most advisor specialization |
| **Top Advisory Type** | The most common type of advisory service offered |

### Top Sectors
Number of advisors per sector, helping you identify where advisory expertise is concentrated.
- Examples: *Finance (43), Energy (28), Agriculture (26), Real Estate (24), Health (19), Information Technology/Software/Blockchain (15), Impact (14), Water (12)*

### Top Countries
Number of advisors per country, showing where advisory support is most available.
- Examples: *Kenya (39), Ghana (18), Uganda (16), Nigeria (16), Somalia (16), Tanzania (12), Rwanda (12), South Sudan (7)*

### Sector Breakdown
A detailed breakdown of advisors per sector — mirrors Top Sectors with full sector-level counts.

### Top Advisory Types
The most common combinations of advisory services offered by advisors in the database.
- Examples: *Equity, Debt, Mergers & Acquisitions (17); Equity, Debt (10); Equity (7); Equity, Debt, Grant (4); Equity, Debt, Grant, Mergers & Acquisitions (4)*

### Sector Distribution Chart
A horizontal bar chart visualizing the spread of advisors across all sectors at a glance.

## Possible Issues
- No advisor matches showing — Business Profile matching criteria may be incomplete
- Advisor list appears empty — try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection

## Possible Remedies
- Complete your Business Profile to improve advisor matching accuracy
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
From the Advisor Database, the user can navigate to any of the following:

1. **View Advisor Profile** — Click on any advisor card to explore their full profile and contact details
2. **Book Advisory Session** — Purchase a one-hour advisory session via the Services page
3. **Go to Investor Database** — Explore matched investors in the global investor database
4. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard
