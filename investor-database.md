# Global Investor Database

> Explore and discover investors from the global database matched to your business profile.
> Access the global investor database at [app.capitalconnect.africa/business/investors](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/business/investors)

---

## Sections
- Matching Criteria Overview
- Investor List
- Investor Database Analytics

---

## Matching Criteria Overview
A bar chart and metric cards showing how many investors in the global database match your business across each matching criterion.

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Matches By Country** | Number of investors matching your country of operation |
| **Matches By Sector** | Number of investors matching your business sector |
| **Matches By Growth Stage** | Number of investors matching your current growth stage |
| **Matches By Use Of Funds** | Number of investors matching your intended use of funds |
| **Matches By Ticket Size** | Number of investors matching your funding amount |
| **Your Matches** | The total number of investors that match across all your criteria combined |

> Your Matches is the most important number — it represents investors who match all your criteria simultaneously, not just one.

---

## Investor List
A scrollable list of investor cards filtered to your business profile. Each card shows:

- **Investor Name** — The name of the investor, fund, or institution
- **Investor Match badge** — Confirms this investor matches your profile
- **Funding Range** — The ticket size the investor typically deploys (e.g. *$100K – $10M*)
- **Description** — A brief overview of the investor's focus, strategy, and geographic preferences

> Click on any investor card to view their full profile, including sector focus, investment stage, ESG priorities, and contact details.

## Investor Database Analytics
A dedicated analytics view with deeper insights into the global investor landscape.
> Access via the **Analytics** tab under Investor Database in the left sidebar.

### Overview Metrics
| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Investors** | Total number of investors in the global database |
| **Available Funding** | Total capital available across all investors in the database |
| **Top Business Stage** | The business stage most investors in the database prefer to fund |
| **Top Sector** | The sector attracting the most investor interest |
| **Top Type** | The most common investor type in the database |
| **Top Sub-Sector** | The sub-sector with the highest investor concentration |

### Sectors Breakdown
Shows the number of investors per sector, helping you understand where investor interest is concentrated.
- Examples: *Financial Services, Technology, Food & Agribusiness, Healthcare, Energy & Natural Resources, Education, New Cities, Industrials, Consumer Goods & Services, Media & Entertainment, Services, Utilities, Transport & Logistics, Tourism*

### Charts & Visualizations
| Chart | Description |
|---|---|
| **Use of Funds** | Pie chart showing how investors prefer their capital to be deployed |
| **Investors Per Subsector** | Stacked bar chart showing investor distribution across subsectors |
| **Investors By ESGs** | Breakdown of investors by ESG focus areas (e.g. Climate, Gender Inclusion, Job Creation) |
| **Investors Per Country** | Africa map visualization showing investor geographic distribution |
| **Top 5 Countries** | Bar chart ranking the top 5 countries by number of investors |
| **Investor Type** | Pie chart showing the distribution of investor types (e.g. Angel, VC, PE, DFI, Family Office) |

## Possible Issues
- Investor list shows no results — Business Profile matching criteria are incomplete
- Low Your Matches count — one or more matching fields in the Business Profile need to be updated

## Possible Remedies
- Ensure all five matching fields are complete in your Business Profile (Country, Sector, Growth Stage, Investment Structure, Funds Needed)
- Review your matching criteria metrics to identify which criterion is returning the fewest matches
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
From the Global Investor Database, the user can navigate to any of the following:

1. **View Investor Profile** — Click on any investor card to explore their full investment profile
2. **Review Platform Matches** — Return to the Dashboard to view high-confidence platform matches
3. **Improve Matching Criteria** — Update your Business Profile to improve your match count
4. **Book Advisory Session** — Get expert help identifying and approaching the right investors
5. **Go to Dashboard** — Return to the main dashboard
