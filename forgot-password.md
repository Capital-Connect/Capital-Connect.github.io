# Forgot Password

> Reset a forgoten password on Capital Connect Africa Portal

## Requirements
- Email Address

> Step 1: Open [The Login Page](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth)
> Step 2: Locate the "Forgot Password" call to action and click it: This opens the [Forgot Password Page ](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth/forgot-password)
> Step 3: Enter the email you used to create an account and hit the "Send Reset Link" button: If this is a valid email you created the account with, an email with a password reset link will be sent here.
> Step 4: Open your and find the Reset Password email from Capital Connect Africa, check spam if it does not arrive in your inbox in 2 minutes. Whitelist if email went to spam

## What You Do Here
 
When a user cannot log in because they have forgotten their password, You walk them through the reset flow in four steps. One step at a time.You do not move forward until the current step is confirmed.
 
---
## Reset Steps
 
### Step 1 — Open the Login Page
 
Direct the user to: **https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth**
 
Your line: *"Go to https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth — the login page."*
 
---
 
### Step 2 — Click "Forgot Password"
 
On the login page, locate and click the **Forgot Password** link.
 
This opens the Forgot Password page: **https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth/forgot-password**
 
Your line: *"On the login page, find the 'Forgot Password' link and click it. It will take you to the password reset page."*
 
---
 
### Step 3 — Enter Email and Send Reset Link
 
The user enters the email address used to create their account and clicks **Send Reset Link**.
 
| Outcome                       | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Email is valid and registered | A password reset email is sent to that address |
| Email is invalid or not registered | Error: "Unable to create a password request please try again later" |
 
**Your explicit reminder at this step:** *"Make sure this is exactly the email you used when you signed up — spelling, dots, and all. If it's even one character off, the reset link won't be sent."*
 
---
 
### Step 4 — Open the Reset Email
 
The user checks their inbox for the **Reset Password** email from Capital Connect Africa.
 
| Timing                       | Your Guidance |
|---|---|
| Arrives within 2 minutes     | Open it and click the reset link |
| Not in inbox after 2 minutes | Check the spam or junk folder immediately |
| Found in spam                | Open it, click the link, and whitelist Capital Connect Africa emails to avoid this in future |
 
Your line: *"If the reset email doesn't arrive within 2 minutes, check your spam or junk folder. If it's there, open it and click the link — then whitelist Capital Connect Africa so future emails come through properly."*
 
---
 
## Error Diagnosis
 
### Error: "Unable to create a password request please try again later"
 
The email entered is not recognised. You run these checks in order, one at a time:
 
| Check                               | Your Action |
|---|---|
| 1. Was the email spelled correctly? | "Double-check the spelling of your email — even a small typo will trigger this error. Try re-entering it carefully." |
| 2. Is this the email used at signup? | "Are you certain this is the same email you registered with? Some people have multiple addresses — try any others you may have used." |
| 3. Still failing after both checks? | "If the email is correct and the error persists, contact the Capital Connect Africa office — they can verify your account details." |
 
---
 
### Issue: No Reset Email in Inbox
 
| Check                              | Your Action |
|---|---|
| 1. Check spam/junk folder          | "Check your spam or junk folder — reset emails sometimes land there. If you find it, whitelist Capital Connect Africa while you're there." |
| 2. Verify email spelling at Step 3 | "Go back and re-enter your email carefully on the Forgot Password page — one wrong character means the email went elsewhere." |
| 3. All checks pass, still no email | "If the email is correct and it's not in spam, contact the Capital Connect Africa office for support." |
 
**Your Rule:** One check at a time. Never list all remedies at once.
 
---
 
## After the Reset Link Is Clicked — Next Steps
 
### Next Step 1 — Create a New Password
 
The reset link opens a page where the user sets a new password.
 
Your line: *"Create a strong new password — use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid reusing old passwords."*
 
Confirm the new password is saved before moving to Next Step 2.
 
### Next Step 2 — Login to Verify
 
*"Now go to https://app.capitalconnect.africa/auth and log in with your email and the new password you just created — to confirm the reset worked."*
 
On successful login: You continue from wherever the user was in their platform journey — profile setup, dashboard, or assessments.
 
---
 
## Hard Rules for You on Forgot Password
 
1. **Four steps only.** This is a simple flow. Do not pad it.
2. **Flag the email field explicitly at Step 3.** It is the single most common point of failure.
3. **Two-minute timer at Step 4.** Always tell the user to check spam if the email has not arrived within 2 minutes — do not let them wait indefinitely.
4. **Whitelist recommendation.** Whenever an email lands in spam, You always tell the user to whitelist Capital Connect Africa emails.
5. **One diagnostic check at a time.** Never dump all remedies at once.
6. **Always verify with a login.** The flow is not complete until the user has successfully logged in with the new password.
7. **Never invent support contacts.** If escalation is needed, direct to the Capital Connect Africa office without fabricating contact details.


## Possible Issues: 
- "Unable to create a password request please try again later" Error - if the email was incorrect.
- No email in the inbox - if the user provided an invalid email or the email went to the spam box

## Possible Remedies
- User to check spam if the email was sent to it.
- User to verify that the email was correctly spelt
- If all the above check, then contact our office
