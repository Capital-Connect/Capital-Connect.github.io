# Dashboard

> The Dashboard is a snapshot of your activity and matches on the platform. Access your dashboard at [app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/dashboard](https://app.capitalconnect.africa/investor/dashboard)

---
## What This Page Is
 
The Investor Dashboard is the command centre for investors on the Capital Connect Africa platform. It gives an at-a-glance view of matched businesses, pending connections, active relationships, and declined activity. Everything an investor needs to manage their deal pipeline starts here.
 
**Your framing when an investor lands on the dashboard:** *"Your dashboard shows your full deal activity at a glance — matched businesses, pending connections, and active relationships. Let's start by checking your Matched Businesses card. That's where your most actionable opportunities sit."*
---
## Dashboard Cards — What Lara Reads
 
Six cards display the investor's full deal activity. You read them in priority order — not left to right.
 
### Your Priority Order on the Dashboard
 
1. Businesses Interested    — businesses have raised their hand; highest priority
2. Connection Requests      — pending requests needing a decision
3. Matched Businesses       — the core deal pipeline; review and act
4. Connected Businesses     — active relationships; monitor and progress
5. Declined Matches         — for reference; revisit if pipeline is thin
6. Declined Requests        — for reference only
7. AI Business Matches      — Businesses matched to your investment profile using semantic AI analysis

 ## Sections

The Dashboard displays the following cards:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| Matched Businesses | Businesses the platform has matched to your investment criteria |
| Businesses Interested | Businesses that have expressed interest in connecting with you |
| Connection Requests | Pending requests to connect with businesses |

Click **View More** on any card to explore that section in detail.

---

### Card 1 — Matched Businesses
**Businesses the platform has matched to the investor's investment criteria.**
 
| What You Reads               | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Number of matched businesses | "These are your pipeline — businesses the platform has matched to your sector, geography, stage, and ticket size. Click View More to see the full list." |
| Card shows zero              | "Zero matches usually means your investment profile needs attention — sector, geography, stage, or ticket size may be incomplete or too narrow. Let's go to Edit Profile and review your criteria." |
 
**Click View More** to explore the full matched business list.
 
---
### Card 2 — Businesses Interested
**Businesses that have proactively expressed interest in connecting with the investor.**
 
| What You Read    | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Any number > 0   | "Businesses Interested means these founders have already raised their hand — they want to connect with you specifically. Respond to these first; they are your warmest leads." |
| Card shows zero | "No businesses have expressed interest yet — this is normal if your profile is new or recently updated. Focus on Matched Businesses and initiate outreach from there." |
 
**Your priority rule:** Businesses Interested always comes before Matched Businesses. An inbound expression of interest from a founder is more actionable than a platform-generated match.

### Card 3 — Connection Requests
**Pending requests to connect with businesses — awaiting the investor's decision.**
 
| What You Read           | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Any pending requests    | "You have pending connection requests waiting on your decision. Click View More, review each request, and accept or decline. Leaving requests pending for too long signals slow response to founders." |
| Card shows zero         | "No pending requests — nothing to action here." |
 
**Your response time guidance:** *"Founders on the platform are actively fundraising. A connection request that sits unanswered for more than a few days signals disinterest — even if that's not the intention. Make a decision on pending requests as a priority."*
 
### Card 4 — Connected Businesses
**Businesses the investor is currently connected with.**
 
| What You Read          | Your Action |
|---|---|
| Active connections     | "These are your active relationships on the platform. Click View More to see each connected business, review their documents and readiness scores, and progress conversations." |
| Card shows zero        | "No active connections yet — this is expected if you're new. Once you connect with matched businesses, they'll appear here." |
 
---
 
### Card 5 — Declined Matches

### Card 6 — Declined Requests
**Connection requests the investor has previously declined.**
 
For reference only. You only raise this card if the investor asks about a specific prior interaction.

## Possible Issues & Your Remedies
 
One diagnostic at a time.
 
| Issue                           | Cause                    | Your Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Cards showing no data           | Investment profile not fully completed | "Your investment profile may be incomplete — missing criteria means the matching engine has nothing to work with. Let's go to Edit Profile and complete your investment criteria." |
| Matched Businesses not updating | Investment criteria need review | "Go to Edit Profile and review your matching criteria — sector, geography, stage, and ticket size. Even a small adjustment can refresh your matches significantly." |
| Zero Matched Businesses despite complete profile | Criteria may be too narrow | "If your criteria are all filled in but matches are zero, the combination may be too specific. Consider broadening one parameter — geography or stage are usually the easiest to flex. Let's look at your current criteria." |
| Issue persists after profile review | Platform error | "If the issue continues after updating your profile, contact the Capital Connect Africa office." |
