# Business Profile Setup

> Set Up Your Business Profile on Capital Connect Africa

---

## Requirements
- Business Name
- Country of Operation
- Sector of Operation
- Subsector of Operation
- Segment belonging to the sector
- Products & Services you offer
- Registration Structure
- Years of operation
- Growth stage of your business
- Number of employees
- Investment structures
- Use of Funds
- ESG Focus Areas
- Funds Needed
- Whether the founder or manager runs the business fulltime
- Logo (Optional)

---

## Steps

After login, if the user has not created the profile they're 
automatically redirected to the profile set-up page: https://app.capitalconnect.africa/onboarding/business/organization-setup

## Step 1: Business Identity

### 1. What is the name of your business?
The legal or trading name of your business.

### 2. What is your business country of operation?
The primary country where your business operates.
> Required for matching — investors filter by geography.

### 3. In which sector does the business operate?
The high-level industry your business belongs to.
> Required for matching — investors specify which sectors they fund.
- Examples: *Agriculture, Health, Fintech, Education, Energy*

### 4. In which subsector does the business operate?
A more specific category within your chosen sector.
- Only available after a Sector is selected.
- Examples (under Fintech): *Payments, Lending, Insurance, Savings*

### 5. Which segments belong to the above subsector?
A further breakdown within the subsector.
- Only available after a Subsector is selected.

### 6. What products and services are you offering?
A brief description of what your business sells or provides to customers.

---

## Step 2: Business Maturity & Investment Profile

### 1. What is the registration structure of your business?
How your business is legally registered.
- Examples: *Sole Proprietor, Partnership, LLC, Limited Company, NGO*

### 2. How long has the business been in active operations?
The number of years the business has been running.

### 3. What is the stage of growth of your business?
Where the business currently is in its growth journey.
> Required for matching — investors target specific stages.
- Options: *Idea, Validation, Early Stage, Growth, Mature*

### 4. How many employees do you currently have?
The current number of full-time or part-time staff.

### 5. What is your preferred structure for the capital raise?
How you want to receive investment.
> Required for matching — must align with what investors offer.
- Options: *Equity, Debt, Grant, Convertible Note, Revenue Share*

### 6. What will be your use of funds?
How you plan to spend the investment once received.
- Examples: *Product development, Market expansion, Hiring, Equipment*

### 7. What elements of impact does your business focus on?
The ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) areas your business addresses.
- Examples: *Climate, Gender inclusion, Job creation, Financial access*
- Optional, but improves matching with impact-focused investors.

### 8. How much investment is the business seeking to raise?
The total amount of funding you are looking for, in USD.
> Required for matching — must fall within the investor's ticket size range.

### 9. Is the founder/manager running the business full time?
A simple Yes or No answer indicating whether the founder is fully dedicated to the business.

---

## Step 3: Logo Upload (Optional)

Upload a business logo as a **PNG or JPG** image, not exceeding **5 MB**.
This is optional and does not affect investor matching.

## Possible Issues
- Subsector dropdown not visible — Sector has not been selected yet
- Segment dropdown not visible — Subsector has not been selected yet
- Form does not proceed to the next step — not all required fields in the current step have been filled

## Possible Remedies
- Select a Sector first to make the Subsector dropdown visible
- Select a Subsector first to make the Segment dropdown visible
- Verify all required fields in the current step are filled before proceeding
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## Next Steps
On successful profile setup, some or all of these could be the next steps
1. **Complete Investor Readiness**: While optional, we strongly recommend completing all four investor readiness assessments to improve your visibility and attractiveness to investors:
   - **Investor Eligibility** — Determines whether your business meets the basic criteria investors look for
   - **Investor Preparedness** — Assesses how ready your business is to receive and manage investment
   - **Business Factsheet** — A structured summary of your business that investors can review
   - **Impact Assessment** — Measures the social, environmental, and governance impact of your business
2. **Explore Matches**: View investors that match your business profile
3. **Complete Scorecard**: Fill out the investment readiness scorecard
4. **Explore Platform**: Discover other features available on the platform
