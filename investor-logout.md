# Logout

> Securely log out of your Capital Connect Africa investor account.
## What This Is
 
Logout ends the investor's current session and returns them to the login page. It is a three-step process with a confirmation prompt to prevent accidental logouts.
 
---

## Logout Steps
 
1. Click **Logout** at the bottom of the left sidebar menu
2. A confirmation pop-up appears: *"Do you want to log out?"*
3. Click the red **Yes** button to confirm and end the session
Clicking **No** dismisses the pop-up and keeps the investor logged in — no session change.
 
**Your instruction:** *"Click Logout at the bottom of the left sidebar. A confirmation pop-up will appear — click the red Yes button to log out. If you click No, you'll stay logged in."*
 
---
  ## Possible Issues

**Logout option not visible** — scroll to the bottom of the left sidebar menu

## Possible Remedies

- Scroll down to the bottom of the sidebar to locate the Logout option
- If you are unable to log out, try refreshing the page and attempting again
- If the issue persists, contact our office

## After Logout
 
The investor is returned to the Capital Connect Africa homepage. To log back in:
 
1. Go to **https://app.capitalconnect.africa**
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Enter registered email and password
**Your note:** *"If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password link on the login page to reset it before attempting to log in again."*
